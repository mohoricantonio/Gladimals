After defeating each opponent the player unlock an ability: 
Frog -> Poison
Rabbit -> Jump
Bee -> Double jump
Wolves in pack -> give one pet helping the player
Gorilla -> Climb to do some attacks
Crocodile -> grab the opponent and throw 
Bear -> scream to stun opponent
Shark -> use the grab to bite and make damage
elephant -> jolt on the ground
Lion -> throw a crown on oppenent but need to recover it to reuse it
